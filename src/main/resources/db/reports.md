KEY DATAPOINTS  FOR MANAGEMENT VISUALIZATIONS

### Key Data Points and Insights

1. **Total Amount Paid Per User**:
    - Calculate the total amount each user has paid over the entire period.
    - **Graph**: Bar chart showing the total amount paid by each user.

2. **Average Daily Payment**:
    - Determine the average amount paid daily by each user.
    - **Graph**: Line graph showing the average daily payment trend over time.

3. **Payment Frequency**:
    - Analyze how frequently users make payments.
    - **Graph**: Histogram showing the distribution of payment frequencies.

4. **Loan Term vs. Actual Payment Period**:
    - Compare the loan term with the actual number of days users took to complete their payments.
    - **Graph**: Scatter plot or bar chart comparing loan term vs. actual payment period for each user.

5. **Total Revenue Generated**:
    - Calculate the total revenue generated from all users.
    - **Graph**: Pie chart or a single bar showing the total revenue.

6. **User Payment Behavior**:
    - Identify patterns in user payment behavior (e.g., do users tend to pay more towards the end of the loan term?).
    - **Graph**: Line graph showing cumulative payments over the loan term for a few sample users.

7. **Late Payments**:
    - Identify how many users are making late payments (i.e., paying beyond their loan term).
    - **Graph**: Bar chart showing the number of users making late payments.
